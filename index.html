How It Works

  • Stay Still: The canvas will slowly fade to black. When there is no movement, you cannot figure out what is in front of the lens.
  • Start Moving: Wave your hands, walk around, or play the video. Visuals will paint the screen, revealing the motion trails.
  • Customize Themes: Switch render modes (e.g. Neon Dust, Hologram) and shift color schemes instantly using the controls on the left.
  • Fine Tune: Lower the sensitivity to capture micro-movements, or increase decay to leave long-lasting particle pathways.
  • Snapshots: Capture snapshots of your motion designs at any time.
